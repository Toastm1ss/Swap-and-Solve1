Bands We Represent

Benny and the Drums

Benny began playing the drums at age six in his parents house in New Jersey. His music combines drum solos with his incredible voice, leading to an unforgettable experience. His style can be classified as Rock Music with heavy focus on drums.

Hairy Hooligans

The Hairy Hooligans are one of the best known rock bands to come out of Australia. Their music focuses on vocals alongside their legendary bass player, Bearded Dave.

The Jonestown Trombones

The Jonestown Trombones are one of the originators of Ska-Pop. Their music has a heavy focus on instruments like trombones and saxophones that their town of Little Rock, Arkansas has come to love.

Lisa Syncopation

Lisa's career has followed many twists and turns from her start in country, through her pop phase, and she is currently writing a mix of guitar heavy pop songs as well as performing a lot of her older country hits. She was born and raised on a Christmas tree farm in Pennsylvania.

The Jackdaws

The Jackdaws are self-described "Heavy Metal Pirates", one of the inventors of the genre. They began their career in Scotland and have since thrilled the world with their unique genre of music.
